== Description ==

Prespa Travel serves as a child theme of Prespa, inheriting all the features of Prespa. The theme is crafted for travel agencies, travel gurus, digital nomads, influencers, writers, bloggers, photographers and other creative individuals or businesses who wish to showcase their work. Theme demo: https://prespa-travel-demo.nasiothemes.com/
